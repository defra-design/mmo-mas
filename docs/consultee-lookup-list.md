## Decisions that affect the list

- King's Lynn is on your office map but not on the MMO's current published list of coastal offices, so it is left out above. Add if it is a live office.
- The following are estuary or tidal-river authorities that may or may not be in scope: Babergh District Council, Basildon Borough Council, Broadland District Council, Warrington Borough Council. Add if MMO consults up-estuary.
- Greater London tidal Thames boroughs (Bexley, Greenwich, Tower Hamlets, Newham, Southwark, City of London, Lambeth, Wandsworth, Hammersmith and Fulham, Richmond upon Thames, Hounslow, and others to the tidal limit at Teddington) are left out pending a scope decision on London tidal Thames works.
- Environment Agency, Natural England and Historic England are listed once each. Split into area teams only if the invite and routing need that grain.
- Marine Conservation Team (MCT) is an internal MMO team, so it is not in this invite list.
